The Art of the Maedeup

Every garment we create is punctuated by a single, handcrafted knot?”the Maedeup. This traditional Korean knotting technique represents the unseen connections of our lives and the artisanal precision we pour into every stitch.

Using heritage-grade linen and silk, our craftsmen spend hours ensuring each pleat and fold follows the natural rhythm of the body, creating pieces that move with a deliberate, soft presence.
